Honest FAQ

Questions we’d ask too

Can you guarantee ChatGPT will recommend us?

No — and nobody can. AI answers are non-deterministic and the engines change constantly. What we guarantee is measurement: you’ll know exactly where you stand, what to try, and whether it worked. That’s more than any “AI SEO” promise is worth.

Where do the prompts come from?

We generate buyer-intent prompts from your website and category, and you edit the list. Your score only means something if the prompts are ones your buyers actually ask.

Do you write the content fixes for us?

We tell you exactly what to create and where it needs to exist — the specific listicle, thread, or page. Drafting assistance is coming for Growth and Agency plans; execution stays with you or your agency, because that’s who should own your voice.

How is this different from my SEO tool?

SEO tools track ranked links on results pages. AI engines don’t return links — they synthesize answers from sources they select. We track whether you’re in the answer, and which sources put you (or your competitor) there.

What happens after I fix everything?

New prompts, new competitors, and engine updates shift answers every week — that’s why the Monday Report exists. Brands that “finish” usually keep tracking to defend what they won.
